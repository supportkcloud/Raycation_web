Our Story

We believe the best travel memories begin with where you lay your head.

Hospitality is personal

Raycation was born from a simple belief: that where you stay should be more than just a place to sleep. It should be a portal to a place, a culture, a way of life that stays with you long after you've checked out.

Every property in our collection has been hand-selected and personally visited. We don't just curate homes — we curate experiences. From the whitewashed villas of Candolim to the misty farm trails of Neral, each stay is chosen for its character, its host, and its ability to make you feel something real.

Our hosts are the heart of what we do. They're artists, farmers, chefs, and families who open their homes because they genuinely love sharing their corner of the world. When you book with us, you're not just renting a space — you're connecting with someone's passion project.
